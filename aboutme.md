# About me

I have always had a very logical mind and an affinity for sciences and computers, as reflected in my academic record. Combined with a quick grasp and my ability to find connections, I enjoy steadily expanding my knowledge on various topics.

During my studies and from a position as a lecturer I have gained experience in the organisation and execution of university-level teaching.

In my current position I work at the interface between theory and computational sciences, with a strong emphasis on programming.
I develop and extend methods and implement them in a widely used software package. I appreciate that usability and reliability are essential features for users.

Working in a team has taught me about writing software collaboratively and project management aspects. Constantly using Linux machines, I have gained experience with a wide range of computer-related tools and concepts.

My style of work is diligent and self-motivated. I am organised and dependable. Being open and sociable I quickly integrate in a new environment. 

I have a calm, but energetic personality and I am resilient to stress.

Outside of work I have broad interests, maintain a healthy balance by being active and like to expand my horizons.
